About Gwadar

The Pearl of
the Arabian Sea

Gwadar is Pakistan's most exciting emerging destination — a coastal city on the Balochistan coast where the Arabian Sea meets dramatic Makran mountains. As the centrepiece of the China-Pakistan Economic Corridor (CPEC), it is transforming into a world-class deep-sea port city.

Beyond CPEC, Gwadar has always been a natural paradise. Golden beaches, giant rock formations shaped like hammers and sphinxes, active mud volcanoes, and Pakistan's largest offshore island are all within reach. The seafood here — fresh from the Arabian Sea every morning — is among the best in the country.

🏖 Hammerhead Peninsula

Naturally formed peninsula surrounded by sea on 3 sides — Gwadar's most iconic landmark

🦭 Astola Island

Pakistan's largest offshore island — 40km from Pasni, famous for scuba diving and dolphins

🌋 Mud Volcanoes

Chandragup sacred mud volcano — erupts mud not lava, revered by Hindu pilgrims

🦁 Princess of Hope

Natural rock statue named by Angelina Jolie during a UN visit to Pakistan

Top Places to Visit

Six Wonders of Gwadar

01
Sea Level · Arabian Sea
Hammerhead Beach
Gwadar's iconic hammer-shaped peninsula surrounded by sea on 3 sides. Golden sands, clear waters, stunning sunset views. The old city sits on this natural wonder.
02
40km from Pasni · Island
Astola Island
Pakistan's largest offshore island — uninhabited, pristine, famous for scuba diving, dolphins, sea turtles and coral reefs. Accessible by boat from Pasni.
03
Natural Wonder · Hingol
Princess of Hope
A naturally carved rock statue in Hingol National Park — named by Angelina Jolie during her 2005 UN visit to Pakistan. One of the world's most dramatic rock formations.
04
Sacred Mud Volcano
Chandragup Mud Volcano
Revered sacred mud volcano — erupts mud not lava. Pilgrimage site for Hindus en route to Hinglaj Mata temple. Fascinating and completely unique natural phenomenon.
05
Coastal Highway · Balochistan
Kund Malir Beach
One of Pakistan's most beautiful beaches — pristine white sands, crystal-clear waters, on the Makran Coastal Highway. Perfect for overnight camping under the stars.
06
CPEC · Deep Sea Port
Gwadar Port & Marine Drive
Pakistan's largest and deepest seaport — centrepiece of CPEC. Marine Drive runs along the coast with stunning night views. Koh-e-Batil viewpoint overlooks everything.

What to Do

Gwadar Activities

🤿
Scuba Diving
Astola Island's crystal-clear waters, coral reefs and diverse marine life. Beginner and advanced dives available.
🦭
Dolphin Watching
Plumbeous dolphins are common in Gwadar Bay — boat tours run daily from the fish harbour.
🎣
Deep Sea Fishing
Charter boats for tuna, grouper and king fish in the Arabian Sea. Fresh catch for dinner.
🏕
Beach Camping
Overnight camping at Kund Malir Beach under a star-filled Makran sky — magical experience.
🚤
Boat Trips
Pasni to Astola Island boat journey — snorkelling, island exploration and sea turtle spotting.
🏔
Koh-e-Batil Hike
500+ steps to the summit — panoramic views of Gwadar city and the Arabian Sea from the top.
🌅
Sunset at Hammerhead
Gwadar's world-class sunsets from Sunset Park at the tip of the Hammerhead peninsula.
🦞
Seafood Trail
Fresh lobster, prawns, crab and kingfish straight from the morning catch at Gwadar's fish harbour.
Travel Essentials

Practical Information

🛫 How to Get There

  • PIA & Serene Air fly Karachi → Gwadar daily (~1hr)
  • Makran Coastal Highway by road (~7hrs from Karachi)
  • Visa-on-arrival for 50+ nationalities at Gwadar Airport
  • International Airport — direct charter flights available

🏨 Where to Stay

  • PC Hotel Gwadar — hilltop, best views in city
  • Golden Crown Hotel — central, mid-range
  • Luxus Gwadar — near port, modern
  • Kund Malir — basic guesthouses + camping

⚠️ Important Tips

  • Avoid June–September (extreme heat 45°C+)
  • Carry extra cash — few ATMs beyond Gwadar city
  • Respect local Balochi culture and dress modestly
  • Book boat trips 1 day in advance at harbour

💱 For International Visitors

  • USD widely accepted at hotels and major shops
  • Exchange available at Gwadar Airport and PC Hotel
  • 1 USD ≈ 280 PKR (March 2026)
  • Tipping recommended — 10% for guides and drivers
